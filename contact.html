Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install TaskFlow as an app?

On most browsers, you'll see an "Install" button or prompt. Click it to add TaskFlow to your home screen. On iOS Safari, use "Add to Home Screen" from the share menu.

Will my tasks sync across devices?

Currently, TaskFlow stores data locally on each device. Future versions will support cloud sync.

Is my data private?

Yes! All your tasks are stored locally on your device. We never send your data to external servers.

Can I export my tasks?

You can view all your tasks in the browser's Application tab under IndexedDB storage.

What browsers support TaskFlow?

TaskFlow works on all modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Mobile browsers also fully supported.
